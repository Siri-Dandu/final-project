Ingredients

  • 2 five ounce salmon filets
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil and coat with cooking spray.
  3. In a skillet, heat the olive oil and then add the garlic. Cook until brown.
  4. Add in the honey, mustard, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir until thickened.
  5. Place the salmon on the baking sheet skin side down.
  6. Brush the glaze onto the filets. Bake for five minutes and then brush again with the glaze.
  7. Bake for five to ten more minutes, until the fish is cooked through.
  8. When done, the fish will easily flake with a fork. It will also be almost translucent in color. Sprinkle the fish with some fresh parsley and serve.

  • 1 lb 2 oz/500 g mussels, cleaned
