Mood Food

Mood: Happy

Ingredients

  • 6 baby lamb chops (1¼ lbs)
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 3 tbsp drained capers
  • 15 sage leaves
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Rinse lamb chops and pat dry.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, and let rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Choose a skillet large enough to hold all chops in one layer.
  4. Warm pan over medium-high heat and add oil.
  5. When oil shimmers, add anchovies and capers.
  6. Cook, stirring, until anchovies break down, about 3 minutes.
  7. Arrange lamb chops in skillet and fry, without moving them, until brown, about 3 minutes.
  8. Turn them over, and toss sage leaves and pepper flakes into pan.
  9. Cook until lamb reaches desired doneness, about 2 minutes for medium-rare.
  10. Arrange chops on serving plates.
  11. Add garlic to pan and cook for 1 minute, then spoon sauce over lamb.
  12. Serve with lemon wedges.

Mood: Sad

Ingredients

  • 2 Medium to Large Ripe Mangoes
  • 1 Packet of Gelatin (3 tbsp)
  • 1/2 cup of Hot Water
  • 1/3 cup of White Sugar
  • 1 cup of good-quality Coconut Milk

Directions

  1. Make sure your mangoes are ripe—the fruit should be bright orange or yellow and fairly soft.
  2. Scoop out the fruit, including around the stone. Place the fruit in a food processor or blender and blitz to create a smooth mango puree. Leave the mango in the processor/blender. To make Mango Pulp.
  3. In a saucepan, heat up the water until it reaches a rolling boil.
  4. Romeove from heat. While stirring the water with a whisk or fork, sprinkle the gelatin over the surface of the water and stir briskly in order not to have any lumps.
  5. Add the sugar to the hot water/gelatin mixture and stir to dissolve.
  6. Add this mixture to the mango in the food processor/blender. Add the coconut milk. Blitz briefly until ingredients are combined.
  7. Pour into dessert bowls or cups and place in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours (or up to 24 if making ahead of company coming).
  8. Serve cold on its own, or with some fresh fruit.
